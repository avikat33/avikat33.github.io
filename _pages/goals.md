### Goals

This project combines my interests in engineering, math, and solving real-world problems. Before MathQuantum, I knew the basic ideas behind quantum computing, such as qubits, superposition, entanglement, and simple quantum circuits, but this program helped me better understand the mathematics behind quantum science, including linear algebra and quantum algorithms. As I begin studying engineering at the University of Maryland, I hope to continue learning about quantum computing by pursuing the Quantum Science and Engineering minor and getting involved in undergraduate research. I am excited to learn more about this growing field and explore how quantum computing can be used to solve real engineering problems in the future.
